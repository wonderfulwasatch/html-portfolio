The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

Rocky

This inspirational fight story details the struglles of a young man trying to find his place in the world as he pours his all into the sport of boxing. He overcomes many challenges and meets the love of his life. He puts up an impressive fight against a worthy opponent and continues on his journey to be the best.

The Summit of the Gods

This movie tells the story of a man who is endlessly obsessed with the goal of climbing mountains in unique and dangerous ways. His unyeilding will takes him to great heights and to an early grave.

Spare Parts

A light-hearted story about kids from a high school in a small town who compete in a collegiate robotics competition. They have many obstacles to overcome and yet still manage a very impressive showing. The movie has comedy drama, and a happy ending. It is based on a true story.
